Project 3

For this project, I made a visual clock that is more personalized with my everyday life, such as brushing my teeth, taking showers, doing homework, and going to Walmart. I've played around with the hour(), minute(), and second() functions to see how certain arrays are arranged as time moves on. The object elements represent foods that are native to Germany, and the fact that I will be participating in a study abroad program this summer. The salt in the pretzel represents seconds, the bockwurst on the left represents minutes, the chocolate cake on the top represents minutes, and the current day is listed on the top right hand corner, along with the German flag that represents the cultural food being displayed on here.
