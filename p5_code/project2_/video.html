Project 2: Spirographs Go Round and Round

For this project, I'm trying to create a spiral that starts off as a tiny dot, then grows and rotates clockwise as the transformation progresses. Then, it flies off to the top left corner of the screen and comes back to the center of the screen as a normal spirograph. A second spirograph is created that goes twice the speed and a third one spreads through the screen and exits at the right side of the screen. The most difficult part about this project is not getting the push/pop function to work on spirographs since the shape is traced and leaves a permanent path, compared to simple, static shapes where it can be easily transformed over time.
