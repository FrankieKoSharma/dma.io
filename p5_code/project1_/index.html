Project 1

In this project, I've created a couple of brushes that varies by color, line stroke, and opacity, as well as two brush shapes that resemble the Raising Cane's logo in the form of a transparent PNG image. With the Raising Cane's brushes, one brush varies by width, height, size, and opacity, while the other brush varies by opacity and rotation.

Keyboard Legend:
1: Black Pen
2: Blue Raising Cane's Logo Brush - Squash and Stretch
3: Raising Cane's Logo Brush
4: Thick Teal Pen
5: Eraser
6: Strong Blue Gradient Pen
7: Thick Turquoise Pen
8: Fade Away Black Pen
9: Fade In Black Pen
0: Thick Red Pen
X: Clear Canvas
P: Save Drawing to Computer

PLEASE NOTE: These commands only work with regular brushes and erasers, and not the Raising Cane's Logo Brushes. The controls are also case-insensitive, meaning that you can also hold shift and the key to change the brush stroke.
A: Brush Stroke (1px)
S: Brush Stroke (5px)
D: Brush Stroke (10px)
F: Brush Stroke (20px)
